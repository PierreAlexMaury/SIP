SIP Operators

We saw how works the SIP protocol. Now in order to use this protocol we need some operators which offer SIP services. below, some famous operators using SIP [5]:

• Ippi :

The Ippi operator is from France and it is the most used VoIP operator using SIP.

Ippi stands out with many free and innovative services.

Ippi offers apps for iOS on the App Store and Android on the Android Market.

Ippi established technology partnerships with Siemens, Gigaset, Epygi, Nokia, Cisco, Linksys, Counterpath... to facilitate its users the configuration on the hardware and software.

• Wengophone :

Wengo was for many time the main French challenger to Skype. It was created by the founder of Internet Service Provider Oreka

Offers of Wengophone are classic with unlimited plans and purchase prepaid credit to make calls to the outside.

Wengo recently changed its focus to becoming a matchmaking, purchasing and selling related service.

• Gizmo :

Now acquired by Google, Gizmo became Google Voice, and sees the integration of the phone feature in the pages of his account Google.

You can buy credit communications. The addition of a geographic number is paying but offered at competitive rates.

• Sipgate :

Sipgate is from Germany and is the principal SIP operator. Sipgate is also present in Austria and England.

Sipgate offers free SIP account with a SIP address in addition to a German virtual number available if a proof of residence is provided.

Calls between members are free. The other destinations are available at attractive rates.

SIP Softwares

The SIP protocol is used in many applications, especially into softwares to initiate multimedia session (SoftPhone, Video Conferencing and Instant Messenger). Some examples are presented below [5]:

• Ippi Messenger :

The Ippi messenger supports different OS: Mac, Linux, PC.

So the ippi messenger is compatible with SIP and instant messaging MSN, Jabber, Yahoo...

Ippi is also compatible RSS reader, shared whiteboard and allows encryption of calls.

• X-Lite :

X-Lite can run on PC, Mac or Linux.

This telephony software is developed by Xten Networks Inc. Itcan also be used with the NeoPhone service.

The program only has a user platform in English.

• X-Meeting :

X-Metting supports only one OS: Mac.

X-Meeting is quite stable, has good interoperability with other clients and provides many unique features such as the sophisticated Address Book integration.


• SJ-Phone :

SJ-Phone can run on PC, Mac and mobile.

VOIP softphone that allows you to speak with any other softphone running on a PC/PDA, any stand-alone IP-phone, or using Internet Telephony Service Provider.


• Ekiga :

Ekiga supports OS: Linux and recently PC.

It supports HD sound quality and video up to DVD size and quality.

It is interoperable with many other standard compliant softwares, hardwares and service providers as it uses telephony standard SIP.
